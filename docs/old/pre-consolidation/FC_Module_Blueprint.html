Moat

Auto-translate factory order data into EDI X12 and EDIFACT formats. Send and receive EDI with global buyers. Indian factory qualifies for export contracts it previously could not even bid on. Full trading partner onboarding workflow included.

 Every factory. Prevents the #1 cause of buyer relationship breakdowns. Critical
08 L2
Dispatch & Logistics Tracking
MVP
CURRENT STATE

 Every dispatching factory. Eliminates the highest-frequency buyer complaint. High

MVP

FUTURE STATE
Auto e-way bill generation on dispatch. DGFT shipping bill auto-reconciled. LUT bond expiry tracker with advance alerts. IGST refund status monitored in real-time. Compliance calendar shows every upcoming deadline. Zero GST notices from missed filings or incorrect e-way bills.
 Every exporting factory. High willingness to pay — errors cost crores. Critical
12 L3

MVP
