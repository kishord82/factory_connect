5. Channel Strategy Deep-Dive

5.1 The "CBAM Fear" Campaign (Fastest to Revenue)

EU CBAM transitional reporting became mandatory Jan 1, 2026. Most Indian factories exporting steel, aluminium, cement, fertilizers, electricity, or hydrogen to the EU are NOT compliant. This is the fastest revenue channel.

WeekActionContent
Week 1-2Create CBAM compliance landing page with ROI calculator"How much will CBAM non-compliance cost your factory?" Calculator: input export volume + destination, output penalty estimate.
Week 3LinkedIn thought leadership series (founder posts)5-part series: "The CBAM Wake-Up Call for Indian Manufacturers" — real penalty numbers, case studies of factories that lost EU orders.
Week 4First webinar: "CBAM 101 for Indian Exporters"Co-hosted with EEPC India or CII. Free. Record for YouTube. Gate registration for lead capture.
Week 5-6Direct outreach to top 100 steel/aluminium exportersPersonalized email + LinkedIn: "We noticed [company] exports Rs X crore to EU. Are you CBAM compliant? Free assessment."
Week 7-8Free CBAM readiness audit for 20 factories30-minute call. Assess their current state. Show gap. Present FactoryConnect as solution. Close 5-10 pilots.
Expected result: 10-15 paying CBAM customers in 8 weeks. Average deal size Rs 19,999-39,999/mo. Revenue: Rs 2-6L MRR from CBAM alone.

5.2 The Tally Dealer Network (Highest Volume)

There are 25,000+ Tally authorized dealers across India. Each manages 50-200 factory clients. This is the highest-leverage distribution channel for FactoryConnect.

ElementDetails
Partner SelectionTarget top 30 Tally dealers in Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, Bangalore, Ahmedabad (export hub cities). Must have 100+ active factory clients.
Commission15% of monthly subscription for 12 months. On Rs 14,999/mo plan = Rs 2,250/mo/factory = Rs 27,000/year per referral. Attractive for dealers.
Training2-hour online training for dealer staff. Provide demo environment. Sales deck in Hindi + English. WhatsApp support group for partners.
Co-SellingJoint visits to factory clients during Tally renewal season. "Your Tally now connects to global buyers. No extra software needed."
Scale Target30 dealers x 3 referrals/month = 90 leads/month. 30% conversion = 27 new customers/month by Month 6.

5.3 WhatsApp-First Marketing (Unique to India)

Factory owners and managers live on WhatsApp. Email open rates for Indian SMEs are under 10%. WhatsApp message open rates are 95%+.

TacticImplementation
WhatsApp Business BroadcastsWeekly CBAM/export compliance tips. Short (50 words max). Include one actionable insight. CTA: "Reply START for a free compliance check."
WhatsApp Community Group"Indian Exporters Digital Forum" — 500-member community. Share wins, ask questions, peer networking. FactoryConnect moderates but doesn't hard-sell.
Demo via WhatsAppPotential customer sends a sample Tally invoice photo via WhatsApp. FactoryConnect bot processes it and shows: "Here's how this would look as an EDI 810 invoice." Instant wow factor.
Referral via WhatsApp"Share FactoryConnect with 3 factory contacts, get 1 month free." One-tap WhatsApp forward. Viral loop in factory owner networks.
