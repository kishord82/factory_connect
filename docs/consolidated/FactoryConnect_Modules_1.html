LAYER 1 — INTEGRATION & CONNECTIVITY
01 L1
ERP Connector Library
MVP
Current State
Factory data locked inside Tally, Busy, SAP B1, Zoho Books. Every integration needs a custom developer taking weeks and costing lakhs. No two factories share the same data format. Exchange happens via manual exports and email attachments.
Future State
Pre-built plug-and-play adapters for top 5 Indian ERPs. Factory connects in hours with zero custom dev. One standard data model flows automatically into every downstream FactoryConnect module.
 Every factory on Day 1. Non-negotiable foundation. Critical
02 L1
EDI Engine (X12 + EDIFACT)
Moat
Current State
Global buyers mandate EDI 850/856/810 transactions. Indian SMEs have zero EDI capability. Factories lose high-value export contracts solely because of this data format gap — not due to quality or price.
Future State
Auto-translate factory order data into EDI X12 and EDIFACT. Send and receive EDI with Samsung, Foxconn, auto OEMs. Indian factory qualifies for contracts it previously could not even bid on.
 Export-focused factories in electronics, auto, pharma clusters. Critical
03 L1
WhatsApp Order Intake
MVP
Current State
Orders arrive as WhatsApp messages or voice notes. Someone manually re-types them into Tally. Errors are daily. No audit trail. Orders get missed, duplicated, or fulfilled with wrong quantities.
Future State
WhatsApp messages intercepted via Business API. Claude AI parses SKU, quantity, delivery date and auto-creates a structured order. Zero re-typing. Full audit trail from first message to final invoice. Buyer gets instant acknowledgement.
 Every Indian SME. WhatsApp is their current ERP. High
04 L1
API Gateway & Buyer Connect
Phase 2
Current State
Large buyers managing 20+ Indian suppliers log into each portal separately. Procurement teams waste hours daily on manual status checks. No single integration point exists for Indian supplier networks.
Future State
Secure REST/SOAP API gateway. Buyer's SAP or Oracle connects once and sees all Indian suppliers in a single feed. Rate-limited, OAuth2-secured, buyer-specific API keys. Enterprise-grade reliability.
 Global buyers managing large Indian supplier networks. Medium
LAYER 2 — ORDER & OPERATIONS
05 L2
Order-to-Cash Automation
MVP
Current State
PO arrives → manually entered → production on whiteboard → dispatch by phone → invoice typed in Tally → PDF emailed → payment chased manually. 5–7 days, multiple people, daily errors.
Future State
PO received → production auto-updated → dispatch triggered → GST invoice auto-raised → payment link sent. Entire cycle runs in hours. Cash arrives 3–4 days faster per order. Zero manual steps.
 Every factory owner. Immediate measurable ROI on cash flow. Critical
06 L2
Production Scheduling
Phase 2
Current State
Production planned on whiteboards or Excel updated manually. No visibility on machine capacity or shift availability. Factories over-commit delivery dates and miss them, triggering penalty clauses.
Future State
Confirmed orders auto-converted to a production schedule. Machines, shifts, raw materials assigned automatically. Alert fires when a delivery date is at risk. Buyer sees realistic available-to-promise dates upfront.
 Factories with multiple production lines. High
07 L2
Inventory Sync & Alerts
MVP
Current State
Factory accepts orders it cannot fulfill because nobody checked live stock. Buyers get surprise shortage notifications after expecting delivery. Inventory tracked in Excel updated weekly at best.
Future State
Real-time inventory pulled from ERP via connector. Alert fires when stock hits reorder level. Buyer sees live available-to-promise quantities before placing PO. Zero surprise shortages.
 Every factory. Prevents #1 cause of buyer relationship breakdowns. Critical
08 L2
Dispatch & Logistics Tracking
MVP
Current State
After goods leave the factory gate visibility disappears. Buyers call daily chasing shipment status. E-way bill generated manually and often forgotten or generated after goods are already in transit.
Future State
E-way bill auto-generated on dispatch trigger. Shipment tracked via courier API (Delhivery, FedEx, Blue Dart). Live tracking link pushed to buyer automatically. Order auto-closed on delivery confirmation.
 Every dispatching factory. Eliminates highest-frequency buyer complaint. High
09 L2
Pricing & Quote Engine
Phase 2
Current State
Price quotes sent as WhatsApp messages or hand-typed Word docs. No version control. Buyer reverts with changes, nobody knows which version is current. Quote-to-PO conversion tracked in someone's head.
Future State
Professional branded PDF quotes in minutes. Version history tracked automatically. Buyer approves digitally inside the portal. On approval order auto-created with zero manual handoff to production.
 Factories with custom or project-based manufacturing. Medium
LAYER 3 — COMPLIANCE & QUALITY
10 L3
Digital QC Trail
MVP
Current State
Quality checks happen on paper registers. When a buyer raises a dispute the factory has no digital evidence. ISO/IATF audits require traceable records — factories scramble, sometimes fabricating records retrospectively.
Future State
Mobile QC checkpoints at each production stage with photo, timestamp, digital sign-off. Auto-generated ISO/IATF audit PDF per batch. Buyer sees live QC status on their portal. Disputes resolved with incontestable digital evidence.
 Auto, pharma, electronics factories. Required for export quality certs. Critical
11 L3
GST & Export Compliance
MVP
Current State
Export invoices need DGFT compliance, shipping bill reconciliation, e-way bills, LUT bonds, IGST refund tracking. Factories get this wrong constantly. GST notices arrive months later. IGST refund rejections cost lakhs per year.
Future State
Auto e-way bill on dispatch. DGFT shipping bill auto-reconciled. LUT bond expiry tracker with advance alerts. IGST refund monitored in real-time. Compliance calendar shows every upcoming deadline. Zero GST notices from missed filings.
 Every exporting factory. High willingness to pay. Critical
12 L3
Document Management
MVP
Current State
COAs, COCs, test reports, packing lists, shipping docs stored in physical files or scattered email inboxes. Finding a document from 6 months ago takes hours. Buyers receive wrong versions or outdated certificates.
Future State
Centralised document store (MinIO). Every document versioned and auto-linked to its order, batch, or shipment. Buyer requests a COA — shared instantly from the correct order record. Full searchable document trail.
 Pharma API factories, electronics exporters. Legally required traceability. High
13 L3
ESG & Carbon Reporting
Moat
Current State
EU CBAM and global ESG mandates require Indian exporters to report emissions per production run. 99% of Indian SMEs have no idea how to comply. Risk of export bans or buyer delistings from 2026. No Indian SME tool addresses this yet.
Future State
Factory records energy, water, waste per run via mobile form. FactoryConnect auto-calculates carbon footprint and generates CBAM-compliant ESG reports. Reports submitted to buyer sustainability portals automatically.
 Factories exporting to EU. First-mover market — mandatory from 2026. Critical
14 L3
Audit Log & Compliance Trail
Platform
Current State
No digital record of who did what and when. ISO audits require proof processes were followed. Legal disputes over orders or quality have no audit trail. Factories exposed to litigation with zero documentary defence.
Future State
Immutable append-only log of every system action — order created, QC approved, document uploaded, invoice raised — with user identity and timestamp. Exportable audit PDF for ISO auditors. Full chain of custody.
 All customers. Non-negotiable platform foundation. High
LAYER 4 — INTELLIGENCE & AI
15 L4
AI Field Mapper (Claude)
Moat
Current State
Every factory has a different ERP data structure. Mapping fields between factory ERP and buyer SAP takes a consultant 2–3 days per factory. This hidden cost kills scalability — every new customer needs expensive manual effort.
Future State
Claude AI reads both schemas, auto-suggests field mappings with confidence scores, factory confirms with one click. Onboarding drops from 3 days to 3 hours. Scale to 1,000 factories without proportionally growing a services team.
 Every onboarding customer. Reduces cost-to-serve as you scale. Critical

Future State
Live dashboards: on-time delivery rate, QC rejection trends, revenue by buyer, cash collection days, inventory turnover. Single screen shows exactly where the business stands. Positions FactoryConnect as a management tool.
 Factory owners and CFOs. Drives upgrade to higher pricing tier. High
17 L4
Demand Forecasting
Phase 2
Current State

Future State
12 months of order history analysed per buyer to predict next quarter demand. Factory receives advance alert to build stock before the surge. Claude narrates the forecast in plain language for non-technical owners.
 Growth-tier factories. Premium module at higher monthly price. Medium
18 L4
AI Order Anomaly Detection
Moat
Current State
Factories accept orders without verification. Sudden 10x quantity, unknown buyer, price far below market — all accepted. Costly production runs for goods never paid or collected. Fraud is a silent revenue drain.
Future State
Every incoming order scored against historical patterns. Anomalies flagged before factory accepts — unusual quantity, new buyer with no history, price variance beyond normal range. Factory reviews the flag and decides.
 Mid to large factories. Protects against fraud and supply chain shocks. Medium
